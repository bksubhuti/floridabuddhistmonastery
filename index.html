About Us

Nestled in the serene landscapes of Geneva, Florida, Tayzawbatha Vihara Inc. is a non-profit Buddhist monastery founded to promote the teachings of the Buddha (Dhamma). Led by ordained Sayalay and under Principal Officer Daniel Hlaing, we offer a welcoming space for meditation, retreats, and spiritual growth.

Our community fosters lasting peace through mindful practice, drawing inspiration from Theravada traditions.
