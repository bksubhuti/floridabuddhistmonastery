About Us

Located in Geneva, Florida, Tayzawbatha Vihara, Inc. is a non-profit Buddhist monastery founded to promote the teachings of the Buddha. Situated at 851 Cochran Rd, it serves as a sanctuary for meditation and spiritual growth, guided by dedicated monk.
